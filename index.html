The Kitties To Go Story

Cats to go started when I saw two feral kitties on the side of the road. I took them in, fed them, cleaned them, but quickly realized adding them to my already full-house of cats was not possible. So I thought about renting them out to my friends for a few days at a time. It worked out great, and thus, a business was born!
